句子(1)

我愿意成为他的守护天使
I’ll be yan ‘s guardian angel

爱她所爱，思她所思
love all she loves，think all she thinks

为她承担伤痛，度过苦难
undertaken the pain for her，Through all the suffering

无论贫寒或者富贵
whether poor or rich

无论卑贱或者高贵
whether lowly or noble

无论身处乱世，还是神所不顾
whether in trouble times， or be abandoned by gods

我将为她拔剑而战，为他收起翅膀
I’ll draw my sword, spill my blood, fight for her, and fold my wings for her

不离不弃，直至永远！
never leave or forsake，forever and ever！

——彦、炙心《超神学院》
